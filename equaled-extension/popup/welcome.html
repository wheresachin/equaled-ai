Welcome to EqualEd

Production-grade accessibility toolkit for any educational website. Click the extension icon to get started.

🔊

Text-to-Speech

Read any page aloud with word-by-word highlighting and adjustable speed, pitch & voice.

🎙️

Voice Control

Navigate & interact using your voice. Say "scroll down", "go back", "read page" and more.

🎨

Visual Filters

High contrast themes, dark mode, color vision filters (deuteranopia, protanopia, tritanopia).

📖

Dyslexia Support

OpenDyslexic font, letter/word/line spacing controls, reading guide & mask.

🎯

Focus Mode

Spotlight cursor, reading mask, and animation pause for reduced distraction.

👁️

Eye & Hand Tracking

Gaze-based scrolling and gesture-based navigation via webcam — no mouse needed.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Alt+Shift+AToggle Accessibility Panel
Alt+Shift+SToggle Text-to-Speech
Alt+Shift+CToggle High Contrast
Right-click any textRead selected text
